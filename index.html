The most important and famous cars

Mercedes-Benz is a German manufacturer of luxury cars, buses and trucks. It is the oldest automobile company in the world, as its origins go back to Karl Benz creating the first car in January 1886. Months after this achievement, Gottlieb Daimler and engineer Wilhelm Maybach also produced another car, and in 1901 the first Mercedes car was produced.

Porsche is well known to many sports car fans and others, as it is one of the oldest and most luxurious car brands in the world. Porsche continues to provide us with sports and classic cars every year, and its history extends to more than 90 years. Do you think that this brilliant present was easily built? of course not! After many years and attempts of experimentation, failure and success, Porsche has established its name as a luxury German car.

BMW M is a subsidiary of the German manufacturer BMW. The company was established in 1972 and was initially intended to facilitate BMW's racing program which had been extremely successful in the 1960s and 1970s. Over time, the company began to supply BMW with more efficient models, as modifications included engines and other parts. Cars are modified and tested at the company's Nürburgring circuit near the town of Nürburg in the German state of Rhineland-Palatinate.

Toyota automobile production began in 1933 as a division of Toyota Industries dedicated to automobile production under the supervision of the founder's son, Kiichiro Toyoda. Its first vehicles were the A1 passenger car and the G1 truck in 1935. Toyota Motor Corporation was founded as an independent company in 1937.
